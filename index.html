About the Problem

Waste management is a growing concern in our community. Overflowing landfills, improper recycling practices, and environmental degradation are affecting public health and our local ecosystem.

Our Mission

Eco-Action is dedicated to educating residents and promoting sustainable practices. Our goal is to reduce waste, improve recycling efforts, and foster a cleaner, healthier environment for everyone.

Get Involved

Join our initiative to make a difference! Participate in community clean-ups, attend local workshops, and learn how to reduce waste at home. Every small action counts.
